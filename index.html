FAQS

Do I need an appointment at Raki Quick Haircut?

No appointment is required. Walk-ins are always welcome at Raki Quick Haircut for $19.99 CAD (tax included).

For customers who prefer a reserved time, Fast Pass is available as an optional add-on for $6.99 CAD (tax included). Fast Pass allows you to reserve a selected time and skip the wait. Raki Quick Haircut offers both walk-in flexibility and optional priority service.

How long will my haircut take at Raki Quick Haircut?

Most haircuts are completed in about 15–20 minutes.

More complex style changes may take longer.

Does Raki Quick Haircut charge extra for long or thick hair?

Raki uses a pay-by-job pricing model, not simply based on hair length or thickness.

Most standard haircuts are $19.99 CAD (tax included), regardless of whether your hair is long, short, thick, or thin. For major style changes (e.g. long to short), additional time is required and extra charges may apply.

Our team will always confirm with you before starting.

Is the price always $19.99 at Raki Quick Haircut?

Most standard walk-in haircuts are $19.99 CAD (tax included).

For customers who want to reserve a selected time and skip the wait, Fast Pass is available as an optional add-on for an additional $6.99 CAD (tax included).

The total is $26.99 CAD (tax included) with Fast Pass.

Does Raki Quick Haircut offer haircuts for children?

Yes. Raki Quick Haircut offers haircuts for children and adults. Most standard haircuts follow the same pay-by-job pricing.
